1. Content

The yellow area represents the content. The blue line is the border. Currently, there is no padding, so the border hugs the text tightly.

I am the content.

2. Padding

Padding adds space inside the border (between text and blue line).

Uniform Padding (20px)

I have 20px padding on all sides.

Mixed Padding

Top: 10px, Right: 50px, Bottom: 20px, Left: 5px.

3. Border

The border sits between the padding and the margin.

I have a thick 10px blue border.

4. Margin

Margin pushes other elements away. It is the space outside the border.

I have 40px margin.

Notice the grey box (parent) is pushed away from the yellow box by the white space. That white space is the margin.

5. Height & Width Calculation

Theory: Total Width = Content Width + Padding + Border.

Math: 300px (content) + 40px (padding L+R) + 20px (border L+R) = 360px Total Width.

Content: 300x100
